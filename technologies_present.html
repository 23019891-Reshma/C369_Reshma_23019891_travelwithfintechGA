Innovative technologies that are present:

Hyperledger Fabric:

Hyperledger Fabric is a private and permissioned blockchain framework designed.

Benefits:

  • Allows businesses to customize different parts of the blockchain network.
  • Ensures that only authorized participants can access the network.
  • Uses advanced cryptographic algorithms to secure transactions.

Application in "Travel with Fintech":

  • Every transaction is recorded on the blockchain, providing transparency and security.
  • Once a transaction is recorded, it cannot be altered, ensuring data integrity.

Smart Contracts:

Smart contracts are self-executing contracts with the terms of the agreement directly written into code.

Benefits:

  • Automatically execute and enforce the terms of the contract.
  • Operate on blockchain technology, ensuring that they are tamper-proof.
  • All parties can view the contract terms and the execution status.

Application in "Travel with Fintech":

  • Manages the issuance and redemption of loyalty points automatically.
  • Ensures secure and transparent cross-border transactions.
